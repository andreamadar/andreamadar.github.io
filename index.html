Welcome to my site

  • About me

    • My name is Andrea Madar. I was born in Cegled, Hungary in 1984. I came to the UK in 2006 taking a break from my studies at the University of Szeged to see the world and do a BA here (we did not have BA qualifications in Hungary at the time; which is why I studied at one university, then another, and then the first one again); which I completed in English Language Studies at the University of Bedfordshire in 2007. I then finished my MA English Studies at the University of Szeged, Hungary in 2008.

    I am looking forward to studying again from September 2016, starting an MSc Computer Science course.

  • Work

    • I work as an Information Assistant at the University of Bedforshire's Luton Campus. My duties include helping students and staff with using both the physical and the digital library and the computers; looking after the print collection and helping out with the digital repository. Also, unjamming printers.

  • Hobbies

    • I have recently taken up learning to code: I have dabbled in Java and Python, and I've had lots of fun (and, I must admit, a few headaches) building this site from scratch using HTML, CSS and jQuery - please see the 'Resources' section for the full list of courses and material that helped me in my quest.
